About Me

Building tech
that matters

Hello! I'm Mathias, a Stanford student studying Symbolic Systems. I grew up in rural Peru and I'm passionate about using technology and AI to improve access to education and healthcare in underserved communities.

My work sits at the intersection of human cognition, AI systems, and social impact — asking not just what technology can do, but what it should do for the people who need it most.
